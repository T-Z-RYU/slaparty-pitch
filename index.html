// The "X"

What is SLAPARTY all about？

Two characters are spawned in a sandbox level by their creator, Tielong. Suddenly, a bug occurs, and they begin slapping each other endlessly.

Tielong never intended this, but he finds it fascinating. Instead of fixing the bug, he begins building a variety of levels to explore how far this accident can go.

// Core Mechanic

Rally Slap

Usable when the boss is stunned. Two players volley the boss back and forth like a ping-pong ball. The progress bar advances through continuous slapping. The winner smashes the boss toward the losing player.
