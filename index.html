// The "X"

What is SLAPARTY all about？

Two characters are spawned in a sandbox level by their creator, Tielong. Suddenly, a bug occurs, and they begin slapping each other endlessly.

Tielong never intended this to happen. But to his surprise, he found it interesting. Instead of fixing the bug, he starts to build variety of levels just to see how far this accident could go.

Players inhabit these two glitched bodies, slapping their way through Tielong's gauntlet.
