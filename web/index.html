// Thesis Project

A 3D co-op action game where two characters accidentally slap their way through a series of experiments — designed by their creator, Tielong.

03
// Motivation
Why start from a slap.
References Power Slap · Oscars '22
// Motivation

Why the F is slap?

01Slap is insult.

In games, insulting behavior usually escalates tension between players — and produces bad experiences.

02Slap is theater.

Power Slap. Will Smith at the Oscars. Film slaps. They share two things — they are humiliating, and they are LOUD. Slap makes tension before meaning.

// The Question
Where's the balance between insult and fun when slap becomes the core mechanic of a party game?
